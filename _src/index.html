About Me

I have spent the majority of my life striving to make the web a healthier and more democratic place.

My father, a Natural Language Processing Software Engineer, always taught me to value more than just the outside of things. Therefore, I believe the web can be so much more than it is now—both in front of our eyes & behind the glass—and that the only thing holding it back is our collective fear of change.

My mission is to overcome that fear and push society into a responsible information age. I focus on choosing work that does good; whether it’s a local business, a non-profit, a community program, or an international outreach opportunity; I’m always looking for work that inspires me.
